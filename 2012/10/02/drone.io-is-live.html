Drone.io is Live

Posted by Tom Burke on 2012-10-02

Drone.io is now live and ready for business! It is a hosted continuous integration service.

Try it out here: https://drone.io

It’s free to start, no credit card required. Within minutes you can add your existing projects and have them automatically building & testing. Most programming languages and data services are supported, and new features are added constantly.

Please contact us with any questions or suggestions, we’d love to hear from you.
